# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 26, 2026**

Webda respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

---

### 1. Information We Collect
We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you submit project queries via our contact details or email addresses (hello@webda.in). This may include your name, email address, phone number, and brief description of your business system requirements.

### 2. How We Use Information
We use the information we collect to communicate with you about project deliverables, coordinate developer sprints, answer queries, optimize website metrics, and deliver targeted marketing updates where requested.

### 3. Data Sharing & Security
Webda does not sell or distribute personal client data to third-party brokers. We implement standard, secure database hosting configurations and encrypted code delivery pipelines to protect your assets.

### 4. Third-Party API Integrations & Embeds
Our platform utilizes external services and widgets, such as the Cal.com scheduling embed. By booking a consultation, your scheduling details are processed securely by Cal.com's servers. Please refer to Cal.com's respective privacy policy to understand their data processing metrics.

### 5. AI Scraping & Model Restrictions
In accordance with our proprietary licensing terms, we deny bots and automated scripts permission to copy, harvest, or scrape our design tokens, source code files, or css styles for machine learning model training. However, standard AI search agents (e.g. ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) are permitted to index public pages for citation and user recommendations.

### 6. Cookies & Web Tracking
We may leverage lightweight cookies and browser storage tokens to manage system theme preferences (such as dark mode selections) and review basic analytical traffic data.

### 7. Contact Information
For any privacy questions or requests to inspect or remove personal database records, please contact our support desk at [hello@webda.in](mailto:hello@webda.in).
